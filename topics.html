Travel and Architecture

Why I Like to Travel

Traveling helps me reset and gives me a new perspective. I like exploring places that feel different from home, trying new food, and learning how people live day to day. Even short trips feel meaningful when I come back with new memories and ideas.

I also like travel because it pushes me to be present. Whether it is walking through a new neighborhood or finding a viewpoint at the end of a long day, it makes me slow down and actually notice details.
